## Project description
  a first person shooter game in the syle of DOOM of Wolfenstein 3D, it will use a raycaster to achieve the 3d graphics, and your character will
  need to survive against enemies that come to attack you.

## Needs to Have
- character + camera movement
- 3d environement to move in
- enemies
- attack for enemies
- health system
- menu + inventory
- textures for walls and stuff

## Nice to Have
- animation
- cutscenes
- different difficulties
- multiple levels
- like a puzzle or something, something besides just fighting
- different weapons and attacks
- weapon upgrades
- a currency system
- more sophisticated enemies, with like strategies and stuff
- sound design
- different enemy types
- randomly generated rooms
- game mode with infinite enemies and infinitely large room --- randomly generated
- minimap
